Demonstrating responsive background image using srcset and sizes attributes to create responsive images.

Srcset tool was used to generate 4 breakpoints in each of the original images.

Observe how lower resolution images are downloaded when smaller screens are used. Larger resolution images are only downloaded for users with larger screens, saving on bandwidth as well as improving site responsiveness.

On extra large screens, the highest resolution images are downloaded to present the clearest picture without blur or pixelization.

Observe how the images are presented side by side by the use of a media query for larger screens.
